Solve the First Puzzle

Start your application

You and four others are racing to develop valuable technology. Each of you submits a number. We add all five numbers, subtract the total from 50 (the potential harm from rushing), and multiply that result by your number to get your score.

Your goal is to maximize your score. What number do you submit?

No wrong answers — we want to see how you think.

score = (50 − sum of all numbers) × yours

Example: Alice submits 3, Bob 2, Claire 2, Darius 1, Elaine 0 — the sum is 8, so the multiplier is 42. Alice scores 126, Bob and Claire 84, Darius 42, Elaine 0.
